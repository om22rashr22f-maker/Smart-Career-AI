
# Smart-Career-AI
Building AI course project

## Summary

Smart Career AI is an AI-powered career guidance platform that helps university students and fresh graduates discover suitable career paths, improve their CVs, identify missing skills, and receive personalized recommendations for courses and jobs. The project aims to reduce the gap between education and employment using Artificial Intelligence.

---

## 🌍 Background

Many students and graduates struggle to choose the right career or understand what employers expect.

Some common problems include:

- Poor CV quality.
- Lack of career guidance.
- Missing technical or language skills.
- Difficulty finding suitable job opportunity 
- Limited knowledge of the current job market.

As a university student, I have seen many talented people miss good opportunities simply because they lacked guidance. This inspired me to design an AI assistant that supports career development.

---

## 🚀 How is it used?

Users create an account and upload their CV.

The AI system will:

1. Analyze the uploaded CV.
2. Identify strengths and weaknesses.
3. Recommend suitable career paths.
4. Suggest online courses and certifications.
5. Match users with relevant job opportunities.
6. Track learning progress over time.

### Target users

- University students
- Fresh graduates
- Job seekers
- Career development centers
- Universities

The solution can be used as both a website and a mobile application.

## 🧠 Data sources and AI methods

### Data Sources

- Public job postings (LinkedIn, Indeed, company websites)

- O*NET Database

### AI Techniques

- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Machine Learning
- Recommendation Systems
- Text Classification
- Semantic Search

---

## ⚠️ Challenges

The project cannot guarantee employment.

Possible limitations include:

- Keeping job information up to date.
- Protecting user privacy.
- Preventing bias in AI recommendations.
- Handling incomplete or inaccurate CVs.
